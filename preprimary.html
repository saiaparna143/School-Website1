Courses In Pre-primary

We are offer to three schedules

Nursery

Usually 2 to 3 years old

Children typically start nursery around the age of 2, although some may begin as young as 18 months or as old as 3 years, depending on the program and the child's readiness..

LKG

Age Range: Usually 3 to 4 years old

Children typically enroll in LKG around the age of 3, although the specific age may vary depending on the educational institution and local practices.

UKG

Age Range: Usually 4 to 5 years old

Children typically enter UKG around the age of 4, completing their pre-primary education before transitioning to primary or elementary school.
